Title:
  As a plant operator, I want to receive real-time alerts when sensor
  readings exceed safety thresholds so that I can respond before
  equipment failure occurs

Description:
  Operators monitoring multiple systems need push notifications and
  on-screen alerts when any sensor (pressure, temperature, flow rate)
  crosses a predefined safety limit. Alerts must identify the sensor,
  location, and threshold breached.

Story Points:
  5

Acceptance Criteria:
  Alert fires within 30 seconds of threshold breach.
  Alert displays sensor ID, current value, and threshold limit.
  Operator can acknowledge alert from the dashboard.
  Unacknowledged alerts escalate to supervisor after 10 minutes.
